I am a UX Designer based in Singapore and I am constantly seeking out opportunities to combine my passion for design and technology.


With a background in technology consulting, it allowed me to be part of various roles such as Business Analyst and Software Developer, which helps groom me as a better UX designer. It gave me the experience of learning both the technical knowledge and functional aspects that allowed me to be the bridge between business and technology. With my experience as a software engineer, I can also empathize with the developers on what can be achieved by code and come out with thoughtful and executable UI design and wireframes. I look forward to collaborating with and learn from a diverse group of designers to create meaningful and innovative products.


Outside of work, I enjoy good food and good cycling sessions with friends :)
